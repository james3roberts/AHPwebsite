Colorado Native

AHP Construction is a proud native of Colorado Springs. Our journey began in the late 1990s as a framing company, capitalizing on the constant activity driven by the city's military presence. This environment allowed us to become masters of our craft. Initially focusing on building tract homes and custom residences, we soon realized the potential to expand our expertise. This led us to venture into other trades, broadening our skill set and connecting with professionals who share our commitment to excellence. Over the past twenty-five years, we've assembled a dedicated team of specialists. From decks and siding to drywall and roofing, every aspect of our projects is handled by experts in their field. Our extended family includes professionals in HVAC, painting, electrical work, and landscaping. This network of professionals not only ensures the quality of our work but also provides us with a trusted resource for every aspect of our projects. At AHP Construction, we take pride in being your one-stop solution for all your construction needs.
